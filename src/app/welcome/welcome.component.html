About Us!

Welcome to Hotel Eclipse, where modern luxury meets timeless elegance in the heart of Los Angeles. Founded with a vision to create a sanctuary from the everyday hustle, our hotel offers an extraordinary experience inspired by the celestial phenomenon of an eclipse. We are dedicated to delivering unparalleled hospitality, with a focus on excellence, integrity, innovation, and sustainability. Enjoy our luxurious rooms, state-of-the-art amenities, exquisite dining, and stunning rooftop views. Whether for business or leisure, Hotel Eclipse promises a memorable stay that will leave a lasting impression.
